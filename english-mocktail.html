English Garden Mocktail

prep time: 10 minutes

Serves:4

Ingredients










Extras for your drinks



Steps

  1. Halve one orange and squeeze the juice into a large jug.
  2. Cut the lemon, cucumber, strawberries and remaining orange into thick slices, adding to the jug as you go.
  3. Pick and finely slice the mint leaves and add to the jug with the vinegars, mint sprigs and a good handful of ice.
  4. Top up with the lemonade and sparkling water and give it a good stir.
  5. Leave to sit for 5 minutes, then pour into glasses and serve.
